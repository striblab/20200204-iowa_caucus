How Amy Klobuchar performed at the Iowa Caucus

By Jeff Hargarten
Star Tribune February 4, 2020

Sen. Amy Klobuchar's campaign to convince Iowa's caucus voters of her presidential viability ended in TK results on Monday night.

Klobuchar was allocated TK pledged delegates, finishing TK behind TK and TK.

Despiting visit all 99 counties in Iowa, Klobuchar's support was strongest in the TK and TK parts of the state, which is more plainly revealed through precinct-level maps of the caucus results.

The strength of Klobuchar's vote is shaded from less to more.
